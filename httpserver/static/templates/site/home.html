Playground & Sandbox

Explore Senzing SDK from the convenience of your workstation.

Depending on your goal, you may want to use the Playground or you may want to use the Sandbox. The Playground has baked-in features that allow you to examine Senzing without installing anything else on your computer. The Sandbox helps you set up an environment on your workstation to use Senzing via a gRPC server.
